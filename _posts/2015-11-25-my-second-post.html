--- title: Marc Goone layout: post date: 2015-11-24 ---

I discovered Marc Goone about twenty minutes before a plane ride to Portugal, and his shit was on repeat the entire flight. I'll throw out a disclaimer real quick: this usually isn't sunchine-and-care-bears rap. If weird stories, self-loathing, and cynicism doesn't do it for you, it might not be your cup of tea.


That said, he's one of the most honest, humble, and lyrically creative artists I've ever heard, if a bit troubled. I struggle to recommend tracks here because my favorite ones aren't the ones that originally drew me to him. Once I got addicted to his flow I got more adventurous, and now songs I had immediately skipped over on that Portugal trip are my most played. Regardless, I'll give it a shot.


Fan Favorites: Hunger (feat. Futuristic), Dear Mom, Chasing Rainbows

My favorites: Mistake, Alright, 25 Bills

Off the beaten path? Try:Meshuganah, War on Drugs
