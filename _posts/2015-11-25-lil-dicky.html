--- title: Lil Dicky layout: post date: 2015-11-25 ---

I crashed at a buddy's place in Chicago, where I was yelled at for not knowing of Lil Dicky.
"YOU DON'T KNOW LIL DICKY? BRO. LIL DICKY?!" ..Uh...no, and..uh..who?


Turns out this kid is clever as hell and litters self-depricating humor all over his tracks. A couple tracks are more like comedy bits, a couple bang, a couple are personal -- it's a pretty diverse album despite all being considered "anti-rap" (poking fun at the industry) so I suggest listening to a number of tracks.


The song you should probably know by now is "$ave Dat Money," and if you're interested check out the last track on the YouTube playlist for a funny little documentary on the making of the music video, which was done spending no money and just convincing people/companies to participate.


Fan Favorites: $ave Dat Money, Professional Rapper, Lemme Freak

My favorites: Molly, Bruh...

Off the beaten path? Try: Work(Paid for that?), Oh Well, Pillow Talking
